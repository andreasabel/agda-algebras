If `g : hom 𝑨 𝑩`, `h : hom 𝑨 𝑪`, `h` is surjective, and `ker h ⊆ ker g`, then there exists `φ : hom 𝑪 𝑩` such that `g = φ ∘ h` so the following diagram commutes:

```
𝑨 --- h -->> 𝑪
 \         .
  \       .
   g     φ
    \   .
     \ .
      V
      𝑩
```

We will prove this in case h is both surjective and injective.
